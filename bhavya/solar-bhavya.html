Top Enlisted Capacities in January ALMM: Waree Energies & Tata Power Dominate 

Following the latest ALMM release, the total enlisted capacity in January's list, released on the 23rd, reached an impressive 73,572 MW, with 100 companies making significant contributions

This marks a substantial increase from December 2024, which recorded 62,765 MW from 95 companies. This surge reflects a growing commitment to renewable energy and highlights the expanding capabilities of the solar industry. The increase in enlisted capacity demonstrates the industry's resilience and its ability to scale up in response to rising demand for sustainable solutions.

Waree Energies and Tata Power have emerged as market leaders, driving growth and innovation in the solar sector. Waree Energies has shown exceptional performance, consistently providing high-quality solar modules that ensure efficiency and reliability. 

The January 2025 ALMM release showcases a remarkable leap in solar capacity, reaching 73,572 MW from 100 companies, up from December 2024's 62,765 MW from 95 companies. This surge underscores the solar sector's robust growth and resilience. The increase in the number of companies signifies that more players are entering the market, further driving innovation and competition, which bodes well for the future of renewable energy in India.
