India Top wind player Suzlon Reports 91% Revenue Growth, 163% Delivery Increase 

Suzlon reported a stellar 90% year-on-year (YoY) increase in net profit, reaching Rs 386.92 crore for the quarter ended December 31, 2024

The company also saw significant growth in revenue from operations, which rose by 91% YoY to Rs 2,968.81 crore. This compares to revenue of Rs 1,552.91 crore and a net profit of Rs 203.04 crore in the same quarter last year.

163% increase in delivery at 447MW. 91% Increase in Revenue of Rs 2,969 crores. 102% Increase in EBITA at Rs 500 crores. 91% increase in PAT at Rs 388Crores.
